## Summary
This Databricks Community Roundup highlights seven new technical blog posts. Topics include a multi-agent supervisor for hybrid retrieval using Agent Bricks and MLflow, query performance tuning for Databricks Serverless SQL, and a study guide for the Databricks GenAI Engineer Associate certification. Other posts cover best practices for data experimentation on Databricks, a metadata-driven ETL framework, RFM segmentation for retail analytics, and converting stored procedures to PySpark.
