## Summary
The article explores how Delta Sharing enables a unified analytics layer across three geographic regions (EU, US, and APAC) without physically copying data. It addresses the challenge of EU data sovereignty requirements that prevent EU data from leaving EU infrastructure while still allowing global analysts to query all data from a single interface. The solution leverages Delta Sharing's protocol to provide cross-region data access with zero data movement. The piece is aimed at data engineers looking to solve multi-region compliance and analytics challenges.

## Key claims
- Delta Sharing enables a single analytics layer spanning EU, US, and APAC regions without copying data.
- EU data sovereignty regulations require that EU data remain within EU infrastructure.
- Business analysts can query data from all three regions through one unified interface.
- Zero data copies are required when using Delta Sharing for cross-region analytics.
- Delta Sharing's protocol facilitates compliant cross-region data access.
