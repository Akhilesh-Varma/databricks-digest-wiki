## Summary
Databricks is hosting instructor-led Learning Festivals for the APAC and EMEA regions in April 2026, scheduled for April 15-16 and April 28-29 respectively. The events focus on upskilling participants in data and AI topics through immersive, hands-on sessions. Both Udemy and LinkedIn have recognized Databricks as one of the fastest-growing skills for 2026. Community members are asking about certification vouchers, which are noted to be provided in the separate Global Self-Paced Learning Festival rather than the instructor-led events.

## Key claims
- Databricks is hosting an APAC instructor-led Learning Festival on April 15-16.
- Databricks is hosting an EMEA instructor-led Learning Festival on April 28-29.
- Udemy listed Databricks among the 100 fastest-growing global skills for 2026.
- LinkedIn listed Databricks among the Top 10 Skills on the Rise for 2026.
- Certification vouchers are provided in the Global Self-Paced Learning Festival, not the instructor-led events.
