## Summary
The article discusses monitoring Databricks data pipelines, addressing the challenge of blind spots that can cause failures even in well-architected systems. It focuses on achieving full visibility into pipeline health, particularly for medallion architecture implementations using Delta Lake and Spark. The piece highlights the operational risks of inadequate monitoring, such as undetected failures surfacing at critical times. It aims to guide data engineers toward comprehensive observability practices for their Databricks workflows.

## Key claims
- Well-architected Databricks pipelines can still fail silently without proper monitoring.
- Medallion architecture implementations are susceptible to blind spots in observability.
- Delta Lake and Spark job optimization alone are insufficient for production reliability.
- Full visibility into pipeline health requires dedicated monitoring strategies beyond pipeline construction.
- Undetected pipeline failures can surface at critical times, such as late at night, causing operational disruptions.
