## Summary
Databricks is piloting a gamified learning experience called the Databricks Detective Series ahead of its launch at Data AI Summit 2026. The free, 30-minute interactive program places participants in the role of a data detective in a fictional 'Lakeview City,' using Databricks Free Edition and the Genie natural language AI assistant to solve real-world-inspired cases. No SQL expertise or coding knowledge is required; users ask questions in plain English, review evidence, and submit findings. Community members are being recruited to test the pilot and provide feedback before the broader launch. Participants progress through fictional locations and climb a leaderboard as they complete cases.

## Key claims
- Databricks Detective Series is a free, gamified, hands-on learning experience set in a fictional city called Lakeview City.
- The series is being piloted ahead of its official launch at Data AI Summit 2026.
- Each 30-minute session uses Databricks Free Edition and the Genie natural language AI assistant.
- No SQL expertise or memorized code is required to participate.
- Users investigate cases by asking questions in plain English, reviewing evidence, and submitting findings.
- Participants progress through locations such as Lakeview Library, Delta Vaults Bank, and Pipeline Plant.
- Community members who complete the pilot are asked to submit feedback via a form.
