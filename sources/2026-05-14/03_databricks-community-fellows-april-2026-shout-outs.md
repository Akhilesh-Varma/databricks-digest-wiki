## Summary
Databricks recognized its Community Fellows program contributors for April 2026, highlighting top performers by points on a leaderboard. Fellows collectively answered 124 technical questions, contributed 69 forum answers, and met a 90% SLA response-time target during the month. A two-week Bug Bash was also conducted on a new version of the Fellows Hub, a centralized Databricks app used by Fellows to find questions, track impact, and earn rewards. Top contributors including Ashwin_DSA, emma_s, and Louis_Frolio were singled out for both forum activity and Bug Bash participation. The post also invites new community members to apply to become Fellows.

## Key claims
- Databricks Community Fellows answered 124 total technical questions in April 2026.
- Fellows powered 69 forum answers and met a 90% SLA response-time target in April.
- Ashwin_DSA ranked first on the April leaderboard with 66 points, followed by emma_s with 29 and Louis_Frolio with 21.
- A two-week Bug Bash was held in April to test a new version of the Fellows Hub, a centralized Databricks app.
- The Fellows Hub is designed to help Fellows find questions, track their impact, and earn rewards.
- Fellows are described as elite community contributors who directly shape customer experience and provide insights to Databricks product teams.
