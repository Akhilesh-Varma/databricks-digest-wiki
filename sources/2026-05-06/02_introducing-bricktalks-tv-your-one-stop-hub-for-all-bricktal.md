## Summary
Databricks has launched a dedicated 'Bricktalks TV' section within the Databricks Community platform. This hub centralizes all past and future Bricktalk session recordings in one place. Users can access it under the Resources tab to browse sessions and suggest future topics. The announcement emphasizes ease of access and continuous updates as new sessions are added.

## Key claims
- Databricks has introduced a dedicated Bricktalks TV section in the Databricks Community.
- All past and future Bricktalk session recordings will be hosted in one central location.
- The Bricktalks TV section is accessible under the Resources tab in the Databricks Community.
- Users can suggest topics they would like to see covered in future Bricktalk sessions.
