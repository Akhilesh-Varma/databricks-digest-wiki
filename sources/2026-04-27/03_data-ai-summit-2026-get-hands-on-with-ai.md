## Summary
The Data AI Summit 2026 in San Francisco is offering a revamped training program with early-bird discounts. Attendees can save 50% on training and certification exams if they register by April 30. The summit features over 20 hands-on courses covering AI agents, vibe coding, app development, and more, with dedicated training days on Sunday and Monday. Participants who pass Databricks certifications will receive exclusive perks like a customized jacket and access to a Certified Lounge.
