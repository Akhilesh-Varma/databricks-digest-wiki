## Summary
The Databricks Community Pulse for April 27–May 3, 2026 highlights top contributors, technical blog posts, and active discussion threads from the week. Featured content includes articles on Azure Databricks reverse SSH tunnels, a new Excel Add-in for native Databricks data access, and a transition from Databricks One to the Genie UI. Active community discussions covered topics such as Agent Mode limits in the Free Edition, serverless public internet access, and Delta Lake time travel issues. The roundup also spotlights customer stories, marketplace drops, and learning series content.

- Databricks One has been rebranded or transitioned to Genie UI, representing a shift from platform to experience.
- A new Excel Add-in enables native access to Databricks data directly within Excel.

- A free BrickTalk event on Supply Chain Management with Databricks was announced.
- easyJet was featured as a Databricks customer story focused on improving travel experiences.
