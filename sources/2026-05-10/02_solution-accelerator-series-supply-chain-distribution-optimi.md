## Summary
The Supply Chain Distribution Optimization solution accelerator is designed to help manufacturers reduce the cost and complexity of global distribution networks using the Databricks Lakehouse for Manufacturing. It leverages linear programming to optimize product distribution at scale and trains machine learning models to predict supply and demand for better inventory alignment. Apache Spark is used to accelerate model training on Pandas DataFrames, enabling faster iteration on large datasets. The accelerator is delivered as Databricks notebooks containing code, sample data, and step-by-step guidance for immediate hands-on use.

## Key claims
- The accelerator uses linear programming (LP) to optimize product distribution across global networks at scale.
- Machine learning models are trained to predict supply and demand, helping align inventory with customer needs.
- Apache Spark is used to speed up model training on Pandas DataFrames for faster iteration on large datasets.
- The solution is packaged as Databricks notebooks with code, sample data, and step-by-step guidance.
- The accelerator is part of the Databricks Lakehouse for Manufacturing platform.
- A related use case demonstrates accelerating demand planning from 4.5 hours to under 1 hour using Azure Databricks.
