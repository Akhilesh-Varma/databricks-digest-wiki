## Summary
This Databricks community roundup highlights ten recent technical blog posts covering a range of topics. Key subjects include automating Power BI refresh pipelines using Databricks Workflows, query performance tuning for Databricks Serverless SQL, and best practices for Jira data ingestion and safe data experimentation. The roundup also covers a customer story from DEICHMANN SE on omnichannel marketing with a lakebase architecture, the announcement of the Databricks storage ecosystem powered by OpenSharing, and a metadata-driven ETL framework. Additional posts explore petabyte-scale ingestion with Zerobus, Metric Views in AI BI Dashboards, and a new short-form video series on Databricks features.

## Key claims
- Databricks Workflows includes a Power BI task type that enables fully automated Power BI refresh pipelines.
- Databricks Serverless SQL (DBSQL) is Databricks' latest offering for building data warehouses on the Lakehouse.
- Databricks introduced a storage ecosystem powered by OpenSharing to connect on-premises, private-cloud, and hybrid data without copying underlying data.
- DEICHMANN SE uses a lakebase architecture to operationalize customer data for omnichannel marketing across analytics, data science, and e-commerce.
- Zerobus Ingest is designed for petabyte-scale data ingestion and has been detailed in a deep-dive engineering post.
- Metric Views in AI BI Dashboards (Genie) is being covered in a three-part blog series.
