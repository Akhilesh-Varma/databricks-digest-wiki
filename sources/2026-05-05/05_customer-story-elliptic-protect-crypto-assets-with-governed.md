## Summary
Elliptic, a blockchain analytics and digital asset compliance company, used Databricks' Agent Bricks platform to build a governed AI copilot that explains cryptocurrency wallet risk in natural language. The copilot helps compliance teams resolve high-risk screenings twice as fast and reduces the time to complete a Suspicious Activity Report (SAR) by 40%. The solution leverages Managed MLflow for tracing and evaluation, ensuring every response is logged and auditable. Built on Agent Bricks, Foundational Models, Model Serving, and Unity Catalog, Elliptic moved from experiments to production-grade AI agents without building custom governance tooling.

## Key claims
- Elliptic's AI copilot helps compliance teams resolve high-risk screenings 2x faster.
- The copilot reduces the time to complete a Suspicious Activity Report (SAR) by 40%.
- Every copilot response is logged with prompts, intermediate steps, and safety scores using Managed MLflow.
- Elliptic built its copilot using Agent Bricks, Foundational Models, Model Serving, and Unity Catalog on Databricks.
- The copilot generates neutral, report-ready narratives aligned to each client's risk appetite.
- Elliptic scaled from experiments to production-grade governed AI agents without building custom observability tooling from scratch.
