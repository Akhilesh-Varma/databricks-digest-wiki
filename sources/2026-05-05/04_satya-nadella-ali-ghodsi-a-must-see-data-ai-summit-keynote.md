## Summary
Data AI Summit 2026 is scheduled for June 15–18 in San Francisco and virtually. The event will feature a pre-recorded keynote conversation between Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella and Databricks Co-founder and CEO Ali Ghodsi. The summit will offer 800 sessions covering data engineering, governance, analytics, AI/BI, and agents. Key Databricks technologies highlighted include Delta Lake, Unity Catalog, and Genie, with a focus on building AI apps and agents that run in production.
