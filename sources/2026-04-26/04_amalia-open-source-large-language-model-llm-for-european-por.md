## Summary
The Portuguese government has announced the development of Amália, an open-source large language model (LLM) for the European Portuguese language. This initiative aims to preserve national sovereignty, recognize cultural and historical elements, and ensure the control and security of sensitive data. The project will be led by the Ministry of Youth and Modernization and the Ministry of Education, Science, and Innovation. Amália is part of the National Artificial Intelligence Agenda, which will be presented in the first quarter of 2025. The model will contribute to the preservation of the Portuguese language and its variants, and will be designed to process and generate text in various formats.

## Key claims
- The Portuguese government is developing an open-source large language model (LLM) called Amália for the European Portuguese language.
- Amália aims to preserve national sovereignty and recognize cultural and historical elements of Portugal.
- The model will be designed to process and generate text in various formats, including text, image, and video.
- Amália is part of the National Artificial Intelligence Agenda, which will be presented in the first quarter of 2025.
- The project will be led by the Ministry of Youth and Modernization and the Ministry of Education, Science, and Innovation.
