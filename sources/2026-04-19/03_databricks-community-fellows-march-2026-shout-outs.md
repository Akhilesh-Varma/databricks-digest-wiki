## Summary
Databricks Community Fellows is a program where internal experts volunteer to help customers in the community forums. In March, the Fellows answered 115 customer questions, provided 77 technical forum answers, and maintained a 92% response SLA. The program is fueled by a larger squad of elite experts and is looking for more members to join. The Fellows Hub, a centralized Databricks app, has been upgraded to streamline workflows and provide a better experience. The program offers benefits such as influence, visibility, and growth opportunities for its members.
