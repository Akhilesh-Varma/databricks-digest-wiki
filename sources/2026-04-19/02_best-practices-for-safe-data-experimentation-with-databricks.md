## Summary
The article discusses the importance of safe data experimentation in today's data-centric world. It highlights the risks of giving developers and data scientists access to production data, including compliance with regulations like GDPR. Databricks offers innovative solutions to help organizations achieve a balance between experimentation and data privacy.
