## Summary
Databricks has introduced new capabilities in Agent Bricks, a governed enterprise agent platform that runs AI agents directly on business data with the right permissions and controls. Agent Bricks unifies data, models, and governance in one place, allowing teams to move from AI pilots to production agents. The platform offers various features, including Document Intelligence, Custom Agents on Databricks Apps, and Knowledge Assistant. These features enable teams to build, secure, and scale enterprise agents that operate on their data. With Agent Bricks, teams can create an enterprise AI fabric, rather than building one-off AI projects.
