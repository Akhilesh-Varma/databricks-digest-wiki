## Summary
Databricks has announced Document Intelligence, a solution designed to improve the ability of agents to read and process real enterprise documents. The solution is built on three core pillars: Research-backed Accuracy, Enterprise Scale, and End-to-end Simplicity. Document Intelligence can turn messy PDFs into reliable structured data, and it has been shown to achieve high extraction accuracy at a lower cost. The solution runs natively on Databricks and can be used with various tools and frameworks. Document Intelligence has been tested on real treasury bond and OfficeQA-style documents, and it has delivered measurable accuracy gains. The solution is designed to power document-heavy agent workflows on Databricks.

## Key claims
- Agents struggle to read real documents, with even frontier agents scoring below 50 on tasks built from real enterprise PDFs.
- Document processing is the accuracy ceiling, and if extraction from contracts, claims, invoices, and other PDFs is wrong, every downstream agent decision is at risk.
- Document Intelligence achieves high extraction accuracy at 5-7x lower cost, with some workloads seeing nearly 90% lower cost while processing hundreds of millions of clinical notes.
- The solution runs natively on Databricks and can be used with various tools and frameworks, including Lakeflow Connect, Lakeflow Jobs, and Spark Declarative Pipelines.
- Document Intelligence has been tested on real treasury bond and OfficeQA-style documents, and it has delivered measurable accuracy gains for multiple agent frameworks.
