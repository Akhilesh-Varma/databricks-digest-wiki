## Summary
Databricks Lake base was used by a global healthcare giant for a Phase III compliance trial, utilizing its time travel and branching features to ensure regulatory compliance and patient safety. The Lake base Autoscaling transactional engine was used for patient vitals, and its branching feature allowed for isolated and instant creation of a staging branch from the production branch. This enabled the team to test and validate regulatory changes without affecting the production environment. The integration with Unity Catalog provided native access controls and governance. The Lake base branching feature was compared to traditional OLTP restore methods, highlighting its advantages in setup time, storage cost, and auditability.
