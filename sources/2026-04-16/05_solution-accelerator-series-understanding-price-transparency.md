## Summary
The Databricks Lakehouse for Healthcare and Life Sciences offers a Solution Accelerator to help health plans and analytics teams ingest, parse, and analyze Machine Readable Files (MRFs) containing U.S. healthcare price transparency data. These MRF files can exceed 150GB and are complex to process at scale. The accelerator provides pre-built code, sample data, and step-by-step notebook instructions. It leverages Spark Structured Streaming for on-the-fly JSON parsing and Databricks SQL for fast analysis of structured price data. The solution also helps organizations meet Transparency in Coverage regulatory requirements.

## Key claims
- U.S. health plan price transparency data is published as large, complex Machine Readable Files (MRFs) that are difficult to parse at scale.
- MRF datasets can exceed 150GB in size, posing significant performance challenges.
- The Databricks Lakehouse for Healthcare and Life Sciences provides a Solution Accelerator specifically for ingesting and analyzing MRF data.
- The accelerator uses Spark Structured Streaming to parse complex JSON objects on the fly.
- Databricks SQL is used to perform fast analysis on cleansed, structured price data.
- The solution helps health plans meet Transparency in Coverage regulatory requirements.
- The accelerator includes pre-built code, sample data, and step-by-step instructions ready to run in a Databricks notebook.
