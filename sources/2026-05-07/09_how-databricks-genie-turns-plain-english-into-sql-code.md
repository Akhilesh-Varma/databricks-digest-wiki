## Summary
Databricks AI BI Genie is a conversational analytics tool built into the Databricks platform that became generally available in June 2025 and is free for all Databricks SQL customers. It allows business users to ask questions in plain English, after which a compound AI system of specialized agents parses intent, plans execution, retrieves relevant schema context, generates SQL, verifies results, and returns a chart and plain-language summary. The multi-agent architecture is designed to reduce the high failure rate of simpler single-model text-to-SQL approaches. The tool is aimed at reducing the 50–70 percent of analyst time reportedly spent on ad-hoc data requests, freeing data teams for more strategic work. Configuration of 'Genie Spaces' is highlighted as the key factor determining whether the tool is reliable or not.

## Key claims
- Databricks AI BI Genie became Generally Available in June 2025 and is free for all Databricks SQL customers with no extra license required.
- Research from Wren AI found that data analysts in fast-paced industries spend 50 to 70 percent of their time handling ad-hoc data requests.
- Genie uses a compound AI system composed of a chain of specialized agents including intent parsing, planning, retrieval, SQL generation, verification, and summarization.
- A single-model text-to-SQL approach fails frequently in production, and Genie's multi-agent design is specifically built to reduce that failure rate.
- Genie writes and executes SQL against a Databricks SQL warehouse and returns results along with a chart and a plain-language summary.
- The reliability of Genie depends heavily on how well a Genie Space is configured.
