## Summary
Databricks Apps now natively support OpenTelemetry, allowing developers to enable observability once and have logs, traces, and metrics automatically flow into Unity Catalog Delta tables. This integration simplifies the observability setup for applications running on the Databricks platform. The article highlights how this native support reduces the operational overhead of instrumenting applications for monitoring and debugging. By leveraging OpenTelemetry standards, Databricks Apps can interoperate with a broad ecosystem of observability tooling.

## Key claims
- Databricks Apps now natively support OpenTelemetry for observability.
- Enabling OpenTelemetry once causes logs, traces, and metrics to flow automatically into Unity Catalog Delta tables.
- The integration reduces operational overhead for instrumenting Databricks applications.
- The solution leverages the OpenTelemetry open standard to enable broad ecosystem interoperability.
