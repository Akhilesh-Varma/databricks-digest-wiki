## Summary
Registration for the Data AI Summit 2026, the world's largest conference for data, analytics, and AI, is now open. The event will take place from June 15-18, 2026, in San Francisco and virtually. Attendees can benefit from early-bird pricing, offering a 50% discount on general admission until April 30. The summit features over 800 sessions, keynotes from industry leaders including Databricks executives, deep-dive training, and networking opportunities.
