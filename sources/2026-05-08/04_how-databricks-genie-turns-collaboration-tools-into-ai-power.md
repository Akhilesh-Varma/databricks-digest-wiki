## Summary
Databricks Genie is an AI-powered analytics assistant that integrates directly into Slack and Microsoft Teams, allowing business users to query enterprise data in natural language without leaving their collaboration tools. The integration aims to eliminate the workflow interruption caused by switching between communication platforms and separate BI tools. Because Genie operates on top of Unity Catalog, existing permissions and governance policies are automatically enforced, making it suitable for regulated industries. The article argues this shifts Slack and Teams from communication hubs into intelligent operational layers, dramatically improving data culture adoption by meeting employees in interfaces they already use.

## Key claims
- Databricks Genie integrates with Slack and Microsoft Teams to enable natural-language data queries inside those platforms.
- The integration eliminates the need to switch to separate BI tools during collaborative conversations, reducing decision-making latency.
- Genie operates on top of Unity Catalog, meaning existing permissions, governance, and auditability are automatically inherited by the AI assistant.
- The approach is designed to scale data culture by bringing analytics to interfaces employees already know, rather than training them on complex BI tools.
- Slack and Teams are described as evolving from communication platforms into operational intelligence layers and AI orchestration platforms.
- The governed nature of Genie makes it viable for regulated industries such as finance and healthcare where uncontrolled AI data access is not acceptable.
