## Summary
The article is a community retrospective on the Databricks Data + AI Summit (DAIS) 2025, held June 9–12 in San Francisco, which drew 22,000 attendees across 700 sessions. The event featured 11 major product announcements including Lakebase, Agent Bricks, Spark Declarative Pipelines, Lakeflow Designer, UC Metrics, Lakebridge, a Free Edition, and Native Gemini integration, along with general availability releases for Lakeflow, AI BI, and Databricks Apps. Community members shared highlights from the Databricks Community Booth, keynote sessions, and networking moments. The post also teases DAIS 2026, framing it as focused on operationalizing data and AI at scale.

## Key claims
- DAIS 2025 attracted 22,000 attendees and featured 700 sessions with 11 major product announcements.
- Databricks announced Lakebase, a transactional database storing data in object storage in open formats with compute segregation, branching, and copy-on-write clones.
- Agent Bricks was announced at DAIS 2025 as a new AI agent development capability on the lakehouse.
- Spark Declarative Pipelines, Lakeflow Designer, UC Metrics, Lakebridge, and a Free Edition were all unveiled during the Day 1 keynote.
- Lakeflow, AI BI, and Databricks Apps reached general availability at DAIS 2025.
- Native Gemini integration was announced as part of the DAIS 2025 keynote.
- DAIS 2026 is planned for San Francisco and is described as focused on operationalizing data and AI at scale.
