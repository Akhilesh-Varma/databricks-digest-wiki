## Summary
Databricks is advancing its Apache Iceberg support through several major milestones including the general availability of Iceberg v3, Open Sharing capabilities, and unified governance via Unity Catalog. The article positions the catalog layer as the defining element of the next phase of the open lakehouse architecture. These updates aim to improve interoperability, data sharing, and governance across the Databricks platform. Unity Catalog serves as the central governance layer tying together Iceberg-based workloads with open standards.

## Key claims
- Databricks has announced general availability of Apache Iceberg v3 support on its platform.
- Open Sharing capabilities are being introduced to enable broader interoperability with Iceberg-based systems.
- Unity Catalog provides unified governance for Apache Iceberg workloads on Databricks.
- The catalog layer is identified as the defining component of the next era of the open lakehouse.
- Open table formats are central to Databricks' strategy for the open lakehouse.
