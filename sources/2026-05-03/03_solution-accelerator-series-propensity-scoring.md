## Summary
Databricks has released Solution Accelerators focused on propensity scoring for e-commerce and marketing use cases. The accelerators address two distinct problems: estimating customer likelihood to respond to offers using historical sales data, and predicting purchase intent in real time using clickstream events. Batch propensity scores can drive smarter email, SMS, and CRM targeting, while real-time scores power on-site recommendations and cart-recovery interventions. Ready-to-run Databricks Notebooks are provided to help teams quickly turn raw transaction and streaming web data into personalized customer experiences.

## Key claims
- Databricks released Solution Accelerators for both batch propensity scoring on historical sales data and real-time clickstream propensity scoring in e-commerce journeys.
- The batch propensity accelerator uses historical orders, products, and customer data to infer individual preferences and likelihood to respond to offers.
- The real-time clickstream accelerator uses live events such as views, clicks, and cart updates to read in-session purchase intent.
- Batch propensity scores are intended to improve email, SMS, and CRM campaign targeting, while real-time scores power on-site recommendations and promotions.
- Both accelerators are delivered as ready-to-run Databricks Notebooks that transform raw data into reusable feature sets and scoring pipelines.
- The accelerators target lifecycle, CRM, and marketing ops teams as well as e-commerce, growth, and product teams.
