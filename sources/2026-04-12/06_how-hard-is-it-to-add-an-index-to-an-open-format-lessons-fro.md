## Summary
The Apache Iceberg community is actively discussing the addition of secondary indexes to the open table format. The article explores the technical and governance challenges involved in extending an open format like Iceberg with indexing capabilities. It draws lessons from the community debate to highlight how difficult it is to reach consensus and implement such features in a widely adopted open-source project. The discussion reflects broader tensions between performance optimization needs and the complexity of maintaining a stable, interoperable open format.
