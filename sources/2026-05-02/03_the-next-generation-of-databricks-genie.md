## Summary
Databricks is launching the next generation of Genie, an AI-powered unified interface for business users that consolidates governed data, dashboards, apps, and chat. The new Genie can route queries across certified Genie Spaces, dashboards, and apps, reusing vetted logic already built by teams. It supports connections to enterprise knowledge stores such as Google Drive and SharePoint via MCP-backed systems, managed through Unity Catalog and the AI Gateway. The product absorbs the functionality previously known as Databricks One, serving as a global account-level home organized by business domain. Genie is now available on web, iOS, and Android, and features enhanced agentic reasoning backed by new agent architecture and the latest reasoning models.

## Key claims
- Databricks is introducing a next-generation Genie that unifies governed data, dashboards, apps, and chat in a single AI-powered interface.
- Genie can route chat queries across certified Genie Spaces, dashboards, queries, and apps, reusing previously vetted logic.
- Enterprise knowledge stores such as Google Drive and SharePoint can be connected to Genie via MCP-backed systems, managed through Unity Catalog and the AI Gateway.
- The business experience formerly known as Databricks One has been merged into Genie as a global, account-level home organized by business domain.
- Genie is now available on web browsers and via native iOS and Android apps.
- New agent architecture and the latest reasoning models enable Genie to handle open-ended, multi-domain questions combining structured data and connected knowledge sources.
