## Summary
The Databricks Community is inviting members to submit blogs and videos for greater exposure and impact. Blog posts can be submitted through the Knowledge Sharing Hub, where they are reviewed and featured upon approval. Videos can be submitted by emailing the community team. Several community members have expressed interest in contributing technical blogs and have been directed to a direct message process for next steps. The initiative aims to foster a culture of knowledge sharing and collaboration among data enthusiasts.

## Key claims
- Databricks Community members can submit blog posts to the Knowledge Sharing Hub for review and potential featuring on the platform.
- Video submissions should be sent via email to the Databricks Community team for review.
- Submitted content is reviewed by the Databricks team before being featured.
- Community members interested in contributing technical blogs are guided through the process via direct messages.
- The program aims to foster knowledge sharing and collaboration within the Databricks Community.
