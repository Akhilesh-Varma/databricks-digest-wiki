## Summary
DecisionBox is an autonomous AI agent that runs data discovery on a user's data warehouse, connecting to the warehouse, deciding what to investigate, writing and executing SQL, and shipping validated insights and ranked recommendations. The agent is designed to work with any team that makes decisions based on data and provides full transparency, with every SQL query, reasoning step, and decision logged and visible. DecisionBox is open source, self-hostable, and works with any major warehouse and LLM, including local models via Ollama. The agent can be tuned to a specific industry and allows users to swap or author domain packs without touching the core code.
