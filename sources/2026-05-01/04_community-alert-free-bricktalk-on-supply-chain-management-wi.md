## Summary
Databricks is hosting a free community BrickTalk event on Thursday, May 7th focused on supply chain management. The session, titled 'Running Supply Chain Operations on Databricks: From Dashboards to Agents,' will demonstrate a unified Control Tower concept integrating real-time inventory visibility and AI-powered demand forecasting. Attendees will see Databricks AI BI Genie Rooms and multi-agent Supervisor workflows in action, including natural language querying and autonomous fulfillment planning. The event is part of the BrickTalks community series designed to connect customers with Databricks experts through real-world demos and use cases.

## Key claims
- The BrickTalk event is scheduled for Thursday, May 7th at 9:00 am PT / 12:00 pm ET.
- The session will demonstrate a unified Control Tower for supply chain operations combining real-time inventory visibility and AI-powered demand forecasting.
- Databricks AI BI Genie Rooms will be showcased for answering natural language queries grounded in live data.
- Multi-agent Supervisor workflows will be demonstrated autonomously processing inbound requests to generate fulfillment plans.
- BrickTalks is a community event series where Databricks experts share real-world use cases, demos, and practical insights.
- The event is free and community-sponsored.
