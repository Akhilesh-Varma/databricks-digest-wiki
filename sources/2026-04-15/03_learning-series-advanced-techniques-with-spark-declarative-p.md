## Summary
Databricks Academy has launched a new course, 'Advanced Techniques with Spark Declarative Pipelines,' which replaces the previous 'Databricks Streaming and Lakeflow Spark Declarative Pipelines' course. It now serves as Course 1 in the Advanced Data Engineering with Databricks series. The course covers topics including multi-source pipeline construction, Liquid Clustering, Data Quality checks, AUTO CDC for SCD Type 2, Delta Sinks, Iceberg reads via Delta UniForm, and quarantine pipeline design. It is free, two hours long, and targets professional-level data engineers working on the Databricks Data Intelligence Platform.

## Key claims
- The course 'Advanced Techniques with Spark Declarative Pipelines' officially replaces 'Databricks Streaming and Lakeflow Spark Declarative Pipelines' in the Databricks Academy curriculum.
- The new course is Course 1 in the Advanced Data Engineering with Databricks series.
- The course teaches AUTO CDC INTO for SCD Type 2 pipelines to track historical events.
- Delta UniForm is used to enable Iceberg reads for cross-platform analytics.
- The course covers quarantine pipeline design to safely route invalid records and manage schema evolution.
- The course is free, runs approximately 2 hours, and is rated at professional skill level.
- The course is designed for engineers who have completed 'Build Data Pipelines with Lakeflow Spark Declarative Pipelines' or have equivalent experience.
