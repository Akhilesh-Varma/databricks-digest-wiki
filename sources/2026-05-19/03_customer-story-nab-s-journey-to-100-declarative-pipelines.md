## Summary
National Australia Bank (NAB) is modernizing its enterprise data platform by standardizing on Spark Declarative Pipelines using Databricks Lakeflow, replacing hand-written Spark and legacy ETL tools. The bank has scaled to 1,800 declarative pipelines used by 300–400 engineers, improving job success rates from 86% to 99.6%. NAB has fully migrated its Bronze layer pipelines and approximately 50% of Silver layer pipelines to the declarative framework. The initiative has reduced transformation complexity by 80% in some pipelines and simplified onboarding by providing a single unified framework. NAB's long-term goal is end-to-end streaming from Bronze to Gold layers.

## Key claims
- NAB is running 1,800 Spark Declarative Pipelines on Databricks Lakeflow across 300–400 engineers.
- Pipeline job success rate improved from 86% to 99.6% after adopting Lakeflow.
- Some pipelines saw an 80% reduction in transformation complexity by moving to a declarative design.
- NAB has standardized 100% of Bronze pipelines declaratively and migrated roughly 50% of Silver pipelines.
- NAB's long-term goal is end-to-end streaming from Bronze to Gold layers.
- Lakeflow replaced multiple custom training frameworks with a single unified pipeline development approach.
