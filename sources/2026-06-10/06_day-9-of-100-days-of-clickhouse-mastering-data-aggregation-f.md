## Summary
This article is Day 9 of a 100-day ClickHouse learning series focused on data aggregation techniques. It covers the fundamentals of aggregate functions, GROUP BY, time-series aggregation, and conditional aggregation in ClickHouse. The author also introduces advanced multi-dimensional aggregation features such as ROLLUP and CUBE. The piece emphasizes why ClickHouse performs well for analytical workloads compared to traditional databases, highlighting use cases like dashboards, monitoring, and real-time analytics.

## Key claims
- ClickHouse provides a rich collection of aggregate functions including count, sum, average, min/max, and approximate distinct value estimation.
- GROUP BY in ClickHouse enables summarization across dimensions such as product category, customer region, and event type.
- ClickHouse supports time-series aggregation functions that make per-minute, per-hour, and per-day analysis simple and efficient.
- Conditional aggregation in ClickHouse allows filtered metrics to be computed in a single query pass, reducing complexity and improving performance.
- Advanced features like ROLLUP and CUBE support multi-level summary analytics for growing organizational requirements.
- ClickHouse is widely adopted for observability, monitoring, and real-time analytics workloads due to its aggregation capabilities.
