## Summary
Databricks is rolling out a change over the coming weeks that enables identity federation by default for all newly created workspaces on AWS. This applies even to workspaces that are not attached to a Unity Catalog metastore. The change is limited to new workspaces only and does not affect any existing Databricks workspaces.

## Key claims
- Identity federation is being enabled by default for all new Databricks workspaces.
- The rollout is expected to complete over the next couple of weeks.
- The change applies to new workspaces regardless of whether they are attached to a Unity Catalog metastore.
- Existing Databricks workspaces are not affected by this change.
