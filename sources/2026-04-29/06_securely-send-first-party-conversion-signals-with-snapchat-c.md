## Summary
Snapchat has made its Conversions API available on the Databricks Marketplace, enabling advertisers to securely send first-party conversion signals directly from their Databricks environment to Snapchat for ad optimization. This integration allows brands to share purchase, sign-up, and other conversion events without relying on third-party cookies or pixel-based tracking. By leveraging the Databricks Marketplace, advertisers can maintain data governance and privacy compliance while improving the accuracy of their campaign measurement. The solution is designed to help marketers close the signal gap caused by increasing privacy restrictions and browser-level tracking limitations.
