## Summary
Databricks announced that its Genie products—Genie Spaces, Genie Code, and Genie—will transition to a pay-as-you-go pricing model on July 6, 2026. Each identified user will receive 150 DBUs of free LLM usage per month, roughly equivalent to $10.50 in the US East region, covering approximately 80–100 Genie questions or 20–30 Genie Code coding sessions. Usage beyond the free tier is billed in DBUs based on the underlying LLM models and agents, with no seat-based fees charged by Databricks. Users are advised to review budget controls available through Unity AI Gateway before the pricing change takes effect.

## Key claims
- Genie Spaces, Genie Code, and Genie will move to pay-as-you-go pricing on July 6, 2026.
- Each identified user receives 150 DBUs of free LLM usage per month, worth approximately $10.50 in the US East region.
- Free usage does not apply to Databricks service principals, only to identified users.
- The free tier covers approximately 80–100 Genie questions or 20–30 Genie Code coding sessions per month.
- Usage beyond the free tier is billed in DBUs reflecting the underlying LLM models and agents.
- Databricks does not charge seat-based fees under the new pricing model.
- Users are recommended to configure budget controls via Unity AI Gateway before July 6, 2026.
