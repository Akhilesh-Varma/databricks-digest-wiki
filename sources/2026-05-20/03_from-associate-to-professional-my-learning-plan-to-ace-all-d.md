## Summary
A Databricks Certified Data Engineer Professional shares a structured five-step learning plan for achieving Databricks data engineering certifications, from beginner Python fundamentals up to the professional level. The plan covers Python basics, Lakehouse fundamentals, Apache Spark development, the Data Engineer Associate exam, and finally the Data Engineer Professional exam. The author draws on 10 years of data engineering experience and 4 years with Databricks, recommending free Databricks Academy courses and practice exams at each stage. A key strategy highlighted is scoring at least 90% on practice tests before attempting the real exam, which helped the author score above 95% on actual exams. The professional exam consists of 64 questions in 2 hours, with 4 non-scored questions mixed in.

## Key claims
- The author has 10 years of data engineering experience and 4 years of hands-on Databricks experience.
- Databricks Academy offers free courses covering Python, Lakehouse fundamentals, Apache Spark, and advanced data engineering.
- The Databricks Certified Data Engineer Associate certification is recommended but not mandatory before attempting the Professional certification.
- The Databricks Certified Data Engineer Professional exam contains 64 questions to be completed in 2 hours, with 4 non-scored questions.
- The author's strategy of achieving at least 90% on practice tests before the real exam resulted in scoring above 95% on actual exams.
- Databricks provides an Apache Spark guide during the Associate Developer for Apache Spark exam, so memorizing syntax is not required.
